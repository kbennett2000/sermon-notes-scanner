2.0.0 (Release)
- New  Scan Library: browse saved scans, basic collections, and details.
- No changes to the classic export flow; existing exports continue to work as before.
- Quad detection: corner angle checks now validate quads (reject overly acute/obtuse corners).
- Improved candidate selection with fallback when angle constraints are violated.
- OCR: selected language is now saved and restored across app launches.
- Cropper: reduced interference from system edge gestures near screen borders (uses system gesture exclusion).
