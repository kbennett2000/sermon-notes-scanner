MakeACopy 2.3.1 (Release Candidate)

- Improved accessibility: new and expanded A11y guidance, including haptic feedback and clearer hints.
- Reorder pages (A11y-friendly): actions to move pages left or right.
- Collections / indexing: completed scans are now automatically placed in a dedicated default collection, making them easier to find.
- Unified rotation handling: more consistent image rotation across the entire workflow (viewing, processing, export).
- OCR auto-rotate improved and clarified: clearer option explanation and better handling of detected rotation during export.

Notes
- This is a Release Candidate build; feedback on camera stability and preview behavior is especially welcome.
